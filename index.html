    Endurance testing:

    Endurance testing is important for determining any undesired changes in the precut properties. Our endurance testing machines are state of the art custom built machines, for testing of various products for upto 10 Lakh cycles with variable load & various frequencies of upto 10 Hz & loading capacities up to 40 Tonnes. The machines are fully computerized, calibrated to record almost every parameter required for efficient testing of the product.

    Load Test Parameters:

    The side swing testing machine is used for testing products by applying various loads of upto 40,000Kgs at deflections ranging upto 10 degrees. The Torsional test determines the strength of the bond of the rubber. Similarly the conical test applies load in the conical plane to ensure the required stiffness of the rubber compound. The components are tested for radial & compressive loads upto 40 Tonnes alongwith pre loads ranging from 5-15 Tonnes at frequencies upto 10Hz. stage.

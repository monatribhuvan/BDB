Introduction

B.D. Bansal Steel industries was incorporated in the year 1962 for manufacturing of rubber based products used in various Industrial & Automotive applications . The company has devoted its attention exclusively for the production of various critical & safety rubber and rubber to metal bonded components and engineering items used in hi-speed passenger rail car coaches, customized as per customer drawings & specifications.

    • Manufacturing

    We a Mumbai based manufacturing firm specialising in rubber and rubber to metal bonded parts namely for the railroad and automotive sectors. BDBSI believes & always strived in continuous improvement & upgrading technology for timely & efficient manufacturing. We have the latest state of the art of DESMA 4 liters, 250 TON rubber injection molding machine. The machine is a fully automatic unit, controlling every single parameter of the process, there by providing fidelity in the highest standards of quality. We are able to produce products of having upto 9 Kgs of rubber.
    These products are extensively used across industries and applications including construction, infrastructure, automobile, electrical applications, and appliances.
